ABOUT ME

My name is Lukhanyo Matshebelele. I am a self-taught Web Developer based in Philippi, Cape Town. I have been writing codes since late 2017 but I only started with my projects in 2020. I am currently working for FunDza Literacy Trust as a Technical Assistant and blogger. I have spent my after school life working and volunteering for various NGO's/NPO's because I love giving a helping hand to those who need it. When my passion for Web Development began, I saw myself becoming part of a team that gives this helping hand to businesses and NGO's that need websites to grow. This portfolio showcases the projects that I have built using the skills that I have acquired since I decided to turn my passion into a career. When I am not coding or writing blogs, you'll find me reading, playing video games or watching movies.
